/tg/ Station 13 - Modules - Types — Var Details - Proc Details

mail
/obj/item/mail

Mail is tamper-evident and unresealable, postmarked by CentCom for an individual recepient.

Vars

generic_goodiesGoodies which can be given to anyone. The base weight for cash is 56. for there to be a 50/50 chance of getting a department item, they need 56 weight as well.
goodie_countHow many goodies this mail contains
postmarkedAdds the Nanotrasen postmark overlay.
recipientWho this mail is for and who can open it Note: this variable may be blanked once set by QDEL.
sortTagDestination tagging for the mail sorter
stamp_maxHow many stamps we can have one.
stamp_offset_xHorizontal offset for stamps.
stamp_offset_yVertical offset for stamps.
stampedAdds stamps based on stamps_max.
stampsList of specific stamp overlay icon_state names.
